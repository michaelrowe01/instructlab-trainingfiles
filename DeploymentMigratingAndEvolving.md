This section is intended for Jazz administrators who want to reconfigure
or rearchitect their Jazz solution. Many different challenges can be met
by following the proper techniques described in this section which seeks
to provide additional guidance and best practices for migrating and
evolving your Rational development environment The Jazz products have
included many online migrations within their upgrades over the past few
releases, but that additional guidance and best practice information is
included within the [Installing and
upgrading](DeploymentInstallingUpgradingAndMigrating) section.
[Migrating CLM to a New Environment](Migrating CLM to a New Environment)
below, describes some of the key migrations, or evolutions that this
section seeks to explain and is a great place to start.
